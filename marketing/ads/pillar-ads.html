Ad 09
UGC — Person Talking to Camera
Video General
Creative Direction
A real person (or AI avatar — like HeyGen) talking directly to the camera. Casual, like they're talking to a friend. In their room or a coffee shop. Not scripted-sounding.
  • Duration: 30–45 seconds
  • Vertical format (9:16)
  • Subtitles always on — most people watch without sound
  • Person: young Indian woman or man, 24–30, relatable look
  • Tone: honest, slightly vulnerable, not salesy

"Okay so I want to tell you about something that actually helped me." [ beat ] "I was going through a really rough patch at work. Felt stuck. Felt invisible. Didn't know if I should quit." [ beat ] "And I couldn't talk to my friends about it because honestly — I was embarrassed." [ beat ] "I found this app called Pillar. It has AI coaches — mine is Kabir, he's like a work-life coach." [ beat ] "I literally talked to him for an hour. And I figured out what was actually bothering me." [ beat ] "It's not therapy. It's not weird. It's just... someone to talk to. Link in bio."

I was stuck. Felt invisible at work. Didn't know if I should quit.
