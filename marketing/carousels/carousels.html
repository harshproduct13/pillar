Slide 2 / 6

Here's the truth

On a first date, we're so busy trying
to impress them —

That we forget we're supposed to be watching them too. The date is not just their interview. It's yours.

Slide 3 / 6

01💬

Do they ask questions back?

Talking about yourself is one thing. Showing curiosity is another.

One-sided conversation = one-sided interest.

02💔

How do they talk about their ex?

If every ex was "crazy" or "the worst" —

The pattern is theirs. Not the ex's.

Slide 4 / 6

03📱

How many times did they check their phone?

Phone on the table is a signal.

Someone else is more important to them right now.

04👀

How did they treat the waiter?

People who are rude to those who can't do anything for them —

That's their real character. Unfiltered.

Slide 5 / 6

The most important one

5

Are you yourself around them — or performing?

If you're already a filtered version of yourself on the first date —
that's not sustainable. Your gut already knows.

Remember this

This isn't about judging them.
It's about choosing the right thing for yourself.
